Our Philosophy: Witnessing Culture,
Creating Connection

Our journeys transform sightseeing into true encounter. MIEscape is operated by Ise-Shima Tourism, an organization born of this very soil. We are not simply guides; we are members of the community, sharing our home with you. We create moments of genuine connection with our neighbors—the fishers, the artisans, and the legendary Ama divers. This is travel that fosters lasting bonds, not fleeting experiences.

Guardians of a Timeless Current: The Ama Divers of
Ise-Shima

In the waters of Ise-Shima, a living tradition has endured for 3,000 years. The Ama, female free divers who gather the ocean's bounty, are a National Living Treasure. Their sustainable fishing methods are guided by an ancient wisdom and a profound respect for the sea, ensuring its resources are never depleted. MIEscape offers respectful opportunities to dive and converse with these remarkable women. Your journey becomes a vital link in preserving their incredible culture for future generations.

Guardians of a Timeless Current: The Ama Divers of
Ise-Shima

For over 1,300 years, the Ise Grand Shrine has embodied the philosophy of "Tokowaka"—the belief that eternity is achieved not through static preservation, but through cyclical renewal. This uniquely Japanese vision of sustainability guides us. To pass on culture, to nurture nature, and to revitalize our community. All our actions are designed to resonate with the soul of this land, becoming part of a regenerative cycle for the future.

A Partnership with Place: Our Commitment
to a Regenerative Future

A journey with MIEscape is built on a deep and abiding promise to our home. We are active partners in shaping a sustainable future for Ise-Shima. When you choose MIEscape, you become more than a visitor. You become a partner in protecting and nurturing the natural and cultural heritage of this extraordinary place.
