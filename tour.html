Tour

Zen & Bonsai Odyssey:
A Premium MIEscape Voyage

In today's fast-paced world, we believe true luxury lies in finding a moment of genuine tranquility. We invite you to step away from the everyday hustle and immerse yourself in the profound spiritual heritage of Japan. Through the timeless art of bonsai and the subtle beauty of "Wabi-Sabi"—the aesthetic of finding perfection in imperfection—you will discover a philosophy that cherishes nature and quiet contemplation. Our experiences are designed not merely as tours, but as meditative journeys into the heart of Japanese spirituality. We wish to share a deeper sense of inner peace and a connection with nature that has been cultivated for centuries, offering you a serene space for reflection and self-discovery.

DAY1

Private
Transfer to MIEscape

Your personal chauffeur greets you at Sendai Station or Airport for a seamless journey. Relax in comfort as we escort you to the serene MIEscape coast.

Culture at
Kanran-tei Teahouse

Enjoy a sake tasting with a traditional meal. Later, inspired by the view, express your creativity in a guided haiku workshop in this elegant, historic setting.

Bonsai
Exhibition with a Master

A bonsai master will guide you through a special exhibition. Discover the stories behind each tree and gain deeper insight into this profound and living art form.

Wabi-Sabi & Traditional Arts

In the exclusive Goza-no-ma room, a master explains the philosophy of wabi-sabi, followed by a captivating private performance of traditional Japanese arts.

An Evening
of Luxury at Shoan

Savor an exquisite kaiseki dinner featuring seasonal, local delicacies. Then, retire to your private room, enjoying the serene atmosphere of this exclusive inn overlooking the bay.

DAY2

Special Viewing
at Zuigan-ji Temple

Visit a national treasure, Zuigan-ji, the most important Zen temple in northern Japan. You will be granted special access to view the brilliantly decorated main rooms.

The Date
Clan's Zen Lunch

Savor "shojin ryori," the traditional vegetarian cuisine of Zen monks. This special lunch is prepared in a style passed down by the historic Date clan.

Cruise the
Magnificent Saga-kei Gorge

Board a private boat to cruise Saga-kei, one of Japan's three most scenic gorges. Witness the dramatic, nature-carved coastline and enjoy the panoramic ocean views.

DAY3

Appreciate
Masterpiece Bonsai at "Teien"

Visit "Teien," a specialty bonsai gallery at the Oku-MIEscape Club House. Appreciate the exquisite, living masterpieces up close, and take the opportunity to discuss purchasing one.

Local
Delicacies at Okushoan

Enjoy a special lunch at "Okushoan." Savor the local flavors of "Oke-sushi," served in a traditional wooden tub, and fragrant "Nori-soba" noodles.

Departure via Private Car

Your private car will provide a smooth and comfortable transfer to Sendai Station or Airport for your journey home, concluding your exclusive MIEscape experience.
